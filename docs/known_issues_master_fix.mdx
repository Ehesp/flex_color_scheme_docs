## Chips, passed in iconTheme is not applied
<Error>Applies to Flutter 3.16 and earlier.</Error>
When passing in `iconTheme` to `Chip`, `ActionChip`, `ChoiceChip`, `FilterChip` or `InputChip`, it is not applied.
See issue [#111828](https://github.com/flutter/flutter/issues/111828). Has a fix in master via [#PR 135751](https://github.com/flutter/flutter/pull/135751), but has not landed in Flutter 3.16.
